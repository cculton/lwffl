Metric Glossary

All sections below use these same manager-focused definitions.

  • Surplus (Cost-Normalized): Player season points minus expected points at the same year + cost decile (snake pick cost or auction dollars).
  • PAB: Points Above Baseline (VOLS-style): player points minus position baseline from starter cutoffs.
  • Hit Rate / Bust Rate: Hit = surplus above 0. Bust = surplus below year+cost-decile 25th percentile.
  • Regret Bin: Opportunity-cost proxy: best points in player’s year+cost decile minus player points (floored at zero).
  • Points per Pick: Average season points produced by drafted QB/RB/WR/TE picks.
  • Points per Dollar: Auction-only efficiency (season points / auction dollars spent) for skill picks.
  • Composite %: Season percentile blend using Surplus, PAB, Hit Rate, and inverse Regret Bin.
