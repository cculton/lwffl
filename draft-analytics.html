Universal metric framework

UniversalSurplus over expected at cost: points minus expected points in the same cost decile (snake: pick slot cost; auction: dollar cost).

UniversalPAB / VOLS-style: points above starter baseline by position-year, flex-adjusted from league lineup settings.

UniversalOpportunity-cost regret: missed-value proxy (snake next-window regret or auction/bin regret).

These three metrics are the backbone of the season and multi-year composite with Hit Rate and inverse Regret.

League context used for calculations

  • Scoring lens: league-default scoring (including 0.2 PPR, completion/incompletion scoring, and return-yard components).
  • Outcome metrics are focused on drafted QB/RB/WR/TE picks because K and D/ST stat fields are incomplete in the source draft-stat file.
  • Core lineup context for baseline logic: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 1 FLEX, 1 D/ST, 1 K.

Actionable interpretation

  • Use position-agnostic surplus if the goal is to reward market exploitation.
  • Use position-conditioned surplus if the goal is to isolate within-position selection skill.
  • In auction years, pair composite scoring with points per dollar and spend-share vs points-share deltas to evaluate budget discipline.
  • In snake years, add turn performance and next-window regret to capture order-driven opportunity cost.
