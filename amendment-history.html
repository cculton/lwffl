AMENDMENT HISTORY

LEGION OF WHOM FANTASY FOOTBALL LEAGUE · 2014–PRESENT

This page tracks passed league amendments and implemented rules, listed from newest to oldest.

Year Amendment – Proposal Category Brief Description Status
2025 Amendment #18 – TE/WR Flex Roster Removed the dedicated TE spot and replaced it with a WR/TE flex position. Passed
2025 Amendment #17 – Permanent Auction Draft Draft Made the auction draft the permanent default format going forward. Passed
2024 Amendment #16 – Re-Proposal – One-Year Auction Draft Draft Used an auction draft for that season only, with permanence reconsidered later. Passed
2022 Amendment #15 – Payout Structure Finances Payout structure changed to: 1st: $450, 2nd: $175, 3rd: $100, 4th: $75, Scoring Title: $100. Passed
2021 Amendment #14 – 3-Division Realignment Structure Moved the league to three divisions and updated schedule and playoff seeding around division winners. Passed
2021 Amendment #13 – Instantaneous Trade Processing Trades Trades process immediately when accepted; LMs handle suspected collusion manually. Passed
2022 Amendment #12 – Choice of Opponent in Playoffs Playoffs Created new playoff structure hwere highest remaining seed selects their playoff opponent in the first round and semifinals. Passed
2021 Amendment #11 – Kicker Scoring Overhaul Scoring Added a 60+ yard FG tier and adjusted scoring and penalties across FG ranges and missed kicks/PATs. Passed
2021 Amendment #10 – FAAB Process Change Waivers First weekly run uses FAAB; the rest of the week is free agent add/drop instead of all-week FAAB processing. Passed
2021 Amendment #9 – Buy-In $50 → $75 Finances Raised the buy-in from $50 to $75 with payouts adjusted accordingly. Passed
2020 LM Decision – Extra IR Slot for COVID Roster Added an additional IR spot to help handle COVID-related player absences. Implemented (LM)
2020 Amendment #8 – COVID Contingency Plan Finances Defined refunds and payout rules for different scenarios if the NFL season was shortened or canceled. Passed
2020 Amendment #7 – Buy-In $40 → $50 Finances Increased the buy-in from $40 to $50 with proportional prize adjustments. Passed
2018 Amendment #6 – Buy-In $20 → $40 Finances Doubled the buy-in from $20 to $40 while maintaining proportional payout structure. Passed
2018 Amendment #5 – “Thursday Night” Trade Rule Trades Allowed accelerated same-day review for Thursday night trades submitted by noon and requested by early afternoon. Passed
2017 Amendment #4 – H2H Tiebreaker Standings Sets tiebreakers as: (1) Points For, (2) Head-to-Head, (3) Points Against, (4) Coin Flip. Passed
2016 Amendment #3 – Division Realignment Structure Realigned divisions by swapping Samuel Wheeler with Evan Turner and Cobey Culton with Matt Dengler. Passed
2015 LM Decision – Expansion to 12 Managers Structure Expanded the league to 12 managers with the additions of Tom Hos and Ryan Turnau. Implemented (LM)
2015 Amendment #2 – Points Wildcard + Kicker Scoring Playoffs Awarded the 6th playoff spot to the highest points-for team (instead of best record) and scaled back kicker scoring. Passed
2015 Amendment #1 – FAAB Implementation + Trade Review Waivers Updated the constitution for ESPN’s $100 FAAB system, shortened trade review from 2 days to 1 day, and clarified FAAB reduction as teams are eliminated. Passed
