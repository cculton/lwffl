2017 Proposal #1 – Add H2H as 2nd Tiebreaker Standings Sets league tiebreakers as: (1) Points For, (2) Head-to-Head, (3) Points Against, (4) Coin flip. Regular-season ranking logic PASSED (8–0)
2017 Proposal #3 – Top Seed Chooses Playoff Opponent v1 Playoffs Early version: top active seed chooses their playoff opponent in the first two rounds. Superseded by 2021 “modern” version FAILED (5–4)
2018 Amendment #1 – “Thursday Night” Trade Rule Trades Adds accelerated same-day review for TNF trades agreed to / submitted by noon and requested by 1 p.m., so deals can clear before kickoff. PASSED (7–0)
2018 Amendment #2 – Buy-In $20 → $40 Finances Doubles the league buy-in from $20 to $40 with proportional payout adjustments. PASSED (10–1)
2020 Amendment – Buy-In $40 → $50 Finances Raises the buy-in again from $40 to $50 as the league pot grows over time. PASSED
2020 Amendment – COVID Contingency Plan Finances Defines refunds and payouts if the NFL season is shut down: separate handling for shutdown before Week 8, between Weeks 8–13, or after Week 13. Pandemic-era safety net PASSED
2020 Extra IR Slot(s) for COVID Roster Adds at least one extra IR slot to accommodate players sidelined by COVID protocols. Commissioner decision IMPLEMENTED
2021 Amendment #1 – Buy-In to $75 Finances Increases buy-in from $50 to $75, further scaling prize pools with league maturity. PASSED
2021 Amendment #2 – Waiver Logic Change Waivers Moves to a hybrid system: first weekly waiver run via FAAB / auction, then rest of the week is open add/drop instead of waivers all week. PASSED
2021 Amendment #3 – Kicker Scoring Overhaul Scoring Adds a 60+ yard FG tier and tweaks points / penalties for various field-goal ranges and missed kicks / PATs. PASSED
2021 Amendment #4 – Remove Automatic Trade-Review Period Trades Trades now process immediately upon acceptance; collusion and bad-faith moves are handled manually by the commissioner if needed. PASSED
2021 Amendment #5 – 3-Division Realignment Structure Realigns the league into 3 divisions and updates schedule & seeding logic (three division winners slotted as top playoff seeds, etc.). PASSED
2021 Amendment #6 – Top Seed Chooses Opponent (modern) Playoffs Modern version of the “pick your opponent” rule: the highest remaining seed chooses its playoff opponent in each of the first two rounds. PASSED
2024 Amendment #1 – Switch to Auction Draft (Permanent) Draft Attempts to permanently replace snake drafts with an auction format (e.g., $200 budget). Fell one vote short. FAILED (stalled one vote short)
2024 Re-Proposal – One-Year Auction Draft Draft Compromise approach: run an auction draft for that season only, with the understanding that permanence will be revisited afterward. PASSED
2025 Amendment #1 – Permanent Auction Draft Draft Second attempt at making auction the default format going forward; this time the permanent switch is approved. PASSED
2025 Amendment #2 – Replace TE with TE/WR Flex Roster Removes the dedicated TE starting slot and replaces it with a TE/WR flex position, increasing lineup flexibility. PASSED (7–0)
2025 Amendment #3 – “Travis Hunter” Scoring Categories Scoring Proposes adding rare D/ST-style scoring for offensive players (pick-sixes, blocked kicks, etc.) in the spirit of Travis Hunter-type usage. FAILED (7–2)
